SUBURBAN FISH FRY

WEBSITE REDESIGN, CREATIVE DIRECTION

Suburban Fish Fry is a restaurant owned by Masonic lodge #740 in Louisville, KY. I was tasked with creating a fresh, straight-forward website that conveys important information.

My mission was to convey information to answer their most common questions: When they are open, what their hours are, and menu items/pricing. Bringing their most important answer front and center: being open every Saturday from March to November. Their new, simple, one-page design allows for easy navigation and brings the information to the forefront.

Customers can now find information easily and can even contact the president for any questions or concerns.
