SARINO

BRAND IDENTITY, CREATIVE DIRECTION

Sarino is a new Italian restaurant owned by the sons of Vincenzo, owner of Vincenzo’s Restaurant in Louisville, KY. Their mission is to add a modern, casual flair to traditional Italian dishes. Their main specialties include fresh pasta and Neapolitan pizza as their main staples.

The goal was to create a fresh brand that separates their concept from their father’s upscale restaurant. Modern typefaces were used while the imagery of a Cornicello tips a hat to their Italian roots.
