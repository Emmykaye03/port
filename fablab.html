FABLAB

WEB DESIGN, BRANDING

FabLab is a concept for an engineering lab and cafe. Users can consult with design engeers about 3-D printing or laser engraving projects. Others can use their self-service lab that has printers and engravers available to the public any time during business hours. While waiting on printing, users can set up additional files on provided computers or sip coffee in the cafe and network with others.
